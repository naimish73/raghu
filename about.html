Our commitment

We are committed to premium quality of products and speedy shipment of cargo to our buyers. We always fill the cargo in our factory unit without any damage and deliver to our buyer in good condition.

We offer best quality at good price because export is not just a business but it is a bond between nations.

Our Strength

Our strength lies in the fact that we are directly connected with the farmers and the government agricultural produce market committee (apmc) so we enable our end users to offer the quality and variety of their choice that too at a very reasonable price.

We are also well connected directly with Mundra port and that port is India’s largest spice exporter.

Raghuveer Trading Jam Jodhpur

We widely known as the Raghuveer jam jodhpur. After learning and gathering knowledge about commodities and spice, we opened Raghuveer trading in 2011. We started our initial business with cotton and then diverted in cumin seed we now mainly work as commission agent of cumin and coriander.

Guru Krupa Trading

In this fame we mainly do commission and business of soyabeans, ground nuts and any kind of pules according to season and arrival. We also work as the commission agent of caster and sesame seed as well.

Raghuveer Seeds

We have recently ventured into the seed business on the demand of farmers to provide good value and effective quality seeds to farmers. We also supply bulk quantity to seed company.
